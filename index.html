About The Company

TSI Inc. is a leading consulting engineering firm that develops and manages critical infrastructure projects. With decades of experience serving government agencies, private enterprises, and communities, we have earned a reputation for delivering innovative and sustainable solutions that enhance the quality of life and promote economic growth.

Comprehensive Civil Engineering Services

  • Transportation Infrastructure: Planning, design, and construction management of roadways, bridges, and transit systems that optimize traffic flow, prioritize safety and enhance connectivity.
  • Structural Engineering: Ensuring the integrity and safety of buildings, bridges, and other critical structures through meticulous design, analysis, and inspection services.
  • Water Resources Management: Developing and implementing effective water management solutions, including hydraulic analysis, design of drainage systems, and flood mitigation strategies.
  • Environmental Engineering: Providing sustainable solutions for environmental challenges, such as wastewater treatment, site remediation, and air quality management.
  • Municipal Infrastructure: Assisting municipalities in the planning, designing, and managing essential infrastructure, including sewer and water systems, waste management facilities, and public works projects.
  • Infrastructure Inspection and Assessment: Conduct comprehensive inspections and assessments of existing infrastructure to ensure safety and compliance and identify potential maintenance or rehabilitation needs.

Client-Focused Approach

At TSI Inc., we prioritize collaboration and client satisfaction. We work closely with our clients to understand their unique challenges and objectives, developing innovative and cost-effective solutions that meet their specific requirements while adhering to all applicable regulations and industry best practices.
